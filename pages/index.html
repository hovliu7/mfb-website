WHO WE ARE

Founded in 2012, Microfinance at Berkeley is a student-run, non-profit business organization that supports small businesses and entrepreneurship in the Bay Area. We are a team of diverse and driven UC Berkeley undergraduates who are passionate about helping the local business community innovate and thrive.

WHAT WE DO

Give Back to the Community

Through consulting and microloan services, MFB is dedicated to helping Bay Area small businesses.

Develop Professionally

Our members gain exceptional training through dedicated resources, ensuring success after graduation.

Create Lasting Friendships

MFB fosters a close-knit community where members form meaningful connections that last a lifetime.
